HOW IT WORKS
◈
Archive bar — Idea bank at the top. Drag cards here to park them. Click to expand/browse. Drag back to reactivate.
○
To Do — Your backlog. Cards show title, owner, and due date.
◑
In Progress — Drag cards between columns to change their status.
✓
Done bar — Completed tasks. Click to expand and review.
⊞
Multiple boards — Click + in the boards bar to add a board. Double-click a tab to rename it. Use ‹ › to paginate when you have many boards.
☁
Google Drive sync — Sign in with Google to save all boards to your Drive automatically. Data is stored in a private app folder only this app can access.
✦
New card — Click the + button or press Ctrl + Enter.
✕
Delete a card — Hold for 2 seconds. A red ✕ badge appears — click it to delete.
📅 Red date = overdue  ·  Orange date = due in ≤3 days  ·  Data auto-saves to localStorage (or Google Drive when signed in).
