HOW IT WORKS
◈
Archive bar — Idea bank at the top. Drag cards here to park them. Click the bar to expand and browse. Drag back to reactivate.
○
To Do — Your backlog. Cards show title, owner, and due date.
◑
In Progress — What's actively being worked on. Drag cards between columns to change status.
✓
Done bar — Completed tasks. Click to expand and review. Cards can be moved back if needed.
✦
New card — Click the + button or press Ctrl + Enter.
✕
Delete a card — Hold for 2 seconds. A red ✕ badge appears — click it to permanently delete.
☀
Theme — Toggle dark / light mode with the button in the top-right corner.
📅 Red date = overdue  ·  Orange date = due within 3 days  ·  Data saves automatically in this browser.
